First of all thank you so much for taking out some time of your busy lives and stopping here to know about my little wandering life!

Hi! I'm Nandana

A 19 year old wandering soul, born and based in Mumbai,India.

Currently a student as well as travel blogger. As a student we can understand how hectic it is to balance travel and studies (mainly college) together! Sometimes it seems like you’re stuck in a vicious cycle. But with full time being a student, I still manage to travel. You know how?

Unlike others our survival list is a little different. It does include breathing, eating, sleeping, drinking, bathing but then the list has one more thing in it and i.e. TRAVELING.

TheWanderHer is basically a site focusing on solo and female travel around the world. On TheWanderHer, I mostly share my travel experiences, travel tips, packing guides, videos, photography from all the destinations I have travelled to.
