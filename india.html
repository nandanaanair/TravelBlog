About INDIA

I myself belong from this incredible country INDIA. India can leave most travellers with a remarkable and lasting impression, regardless of the length of time spent here, and no two visitors will have the same experience. India is a vast region, offering a variety of cultural experiences rich in heritage and beauty. From the snow-dusted peaks of the Himalaya to the sun-splashed beaches of the tropical south, the country has a bounty of outdoor attractions. You can scout for big jungle cats on scenic wildlife safaris, paddle in the shimmering waters of coastal retreats, take blood-pumping treks high in the mountains, or simply inhale pine-scented air on meditative forest walks.
